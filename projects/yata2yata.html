Yata2yata is a mobile trading platform for iOS and Android.

Yata2yata allows users to empty their closets, garages or storage lockers by trading things they have for things they want, while only getting trade offers of equal or greater value. It is a “Heart to Heart” trading platform between friends. The main objective of the app is to connect people with their peers to facilitate a mutual trade between friends.

I wrote this application with one other developer as contract work for SIGMA Development. It was written based on a spec doc given in a clean, scalable manner and is published on both the Apple App Store and Google Play.

The app is written in React Native using Firebase as a backend.
